Who we are & What we do

BIM Factory is a joint venture of three partners

  • Construction planners: rTech engineering GmbH
  • Education and training specialists: Code Factory GmbH

Together we have developed an unique curriculum with the emphasis on practical application and to ensure the 360 degree support of the participants.

We offer state of the art learning support through an electronic learning platform, educational videos and case studies. This allows learning according to individual levels of experience and speeds. Application-oriented group learning and peer-coding makes studying varied and helps participants to stay motivated. Hands-on projects will result in a personal portfolio for each student. What is BIM?

Building Information Modelling (BIM) is a digital representation of physical and functional characteristics of a facility. A building information model is a shared knowledge resource for information about a facility forming a reliable basis for decisions during its life-cycle; defined as existing from earliest conception to demolition.
